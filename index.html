Welcome to SwoleLifts!

What is SwoleLifts?

SwoleLifts is a lifting program meant for anyone who wants to:

  1. Get stronger
  2. Increase Hypertrophy (BIG SWOLE MUSCLE SIZE)
  3. Train all muscle groups
  4. Have a mix of powerlifting, bodybuilding, and cardiovascular training
  5. Live a healthy and active lifestyle
  6. Last but not least, to get swole! 💪 💪 💪

SwoleLifts is a combination of workout programs from:

  1. General bodybuilding
  2. Powerlifting
  3. Circuit Training
  4. High Intensive Interval Training (HIIT)
  5. Yoga

Where to get started?

Programs

SwoleLifts caters to all swole levels (beginner/intermediate/advanced):

  • Beginners - I've never stepped inside a gym before nor do I even know what swole means
  • Intermediate - I can bench/squat/deadlift at least 135lbs with proper form
  • Advanced - I breathe, eat, sleep swole/li>

Equipment

The equipment pre-requisites for any of the programs requires access to either a gym (LA Fitness, Snap Fitness, Anytime Fitness, etc) or a home gym with at least a power rack, barbell, dumbbells, and weights

Accessories

Beginners - Since you're very new to the swole journey, I would advise you to spend as little as possible on equipment. Here's some recommendations to help you get started without breaking the the wallet:

  1. Straps
  2. Wrist Wraps
  3. Belt
  4. Knee Wraps

Intermediate/Advance - Here's the list of accessories that I use:

  1. Straps
  2. Wrist Wraps
  3. Belt (Lever, 10mm)
  4. Knee Wraps
